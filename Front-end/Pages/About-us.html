About Us

Our Mission

We live in a digital world, so why are debt tracking procedures still utilizing traditional methods? Manually updating, tracking and phone calls to defaulters are time-consuming and often unsympathetic. Traditional contact methods also don't get the results they once did.

Mydebtors believe that the future of debt management lies in digital tracking and automation. By adapting the digital tracking process to align with modern habits, debtors can also have access to flexible arrangements which include payments in installments. Our platform also allows you to gain essential insights into your debtors' real- time analytics, which can be vital to optimizing your collection strategy.

Our Purpose

To invent innovative solutions that solve our customer's core challenges

Our Core Customer

Educational institutions that want a platform that can manage and track their debtors
